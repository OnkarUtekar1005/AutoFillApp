# AOC-4 Form — Authoritative Field List

Source: a real filed AOC-4 (ATS SYSTEMS PRIVATE LIMITED, CIN U74999PN2018PTC179514,
FY 2024-25, SRN 1-22959132862). This is the ground-truth field inventory the
tool targets — ~201 numbered items across the form's segments.

Legend for the "Source" column used in the schema:
- **AUTO**  — extractable from the client's financial documents (BS/P&L/audit/board report)
- **MANUAL** — the CA/CS must enter it (not in any document): SRNs, service-provider
  particulars, product ITC/NPCS codes, board resolution numbers, declarations, etc.
- **MCA** — generated by the MCA portal itself (SRN, filing date) — never filled by us
